Essential Formulas for Week 4

Normal Distribution: - Standard Normal: \(Z = \frac{X - \mu}{\sigma}\) - Probability: \(P(a < X < b) = \int_a^b \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}} dx\)

Central Limit Theorem: - Sampling Distribution: \(\bar{X} \sim N\left(\mu, \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\right)\) - Standard Error: \(SE = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\)

Confidence Intervals: - General Form: \(\text{statistic} \pm \text{margin of error}\) - For Means: \(\bar{x} \pm z_{\alpha/2} \cdot \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\) - Margin of Error: \(ME = z_{\alpha/2} \cdot \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}\)

Common Z-values: - 90% CI: \(z_{0.05} = 1.645\) - 95% CI: \(z_{0.025} = 1.96\)
- 99% CI: \(z_{0.005} = 2.576\)
